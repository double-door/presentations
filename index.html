The Problem

Cross-border real estate investment
is broken by distrust.

🔒 Fraud & Scams

Wire fraud, fake listings, impersonation of licensed professionals. The FBI reports $446M+ in RE wire fraud losses annually. Cross-border buyers are prime targets.

🌐 Opaque Markets

Foreign buyers can’t verify licenses, read contracts in another language, or navigate unfamiliar title and escrow systems. Every step is a leap of faith.

⚖️ Legal Complexity

Property law varies drastically between countries. Tax implications, ownership structures (fideicomiso, LLC), and repatriation rules create a compliance minefield.

🗣️ Language Barriers

Contracts, disclosures, and negotiations in a foreign language. Miscommunication kills deals — or creates binding obligations buyers don’t understand.

🤷 No Trusted Intermediaries

No way to verify who you’re working with across borders. Is the agent licensed? Is the escrow company real? Foreign buyers have zero reference points.

💸 Capital & Compliance

Moving money across borders triggers AML requirements. Sellers worry about fund legitimacy. Buyers struggle with proof-of-funds and capital controls.
